Windows doesn't just your passwords in plain text form.

It is stored in the form of something called HASH.

If you don't what a HASH is, read this Wikipedia Article

For the sake of education we will assume that your password is:

Which is a good password and indicated that you used some kind of password generator to generate this. Even then you will be hacked.

After you set this password, it will be went through a hashing algorithm. Which in this case is:

MD4 Hashing algorithm after which it will look like somebody chewed and spat out.

After hashing the password will look something like this:

ca0a1e55c19606f471a5b3ecf8054271

So whenever you type in the password it will again go through the MD4 Hashing algorithm and if the hashes match, you will login.
